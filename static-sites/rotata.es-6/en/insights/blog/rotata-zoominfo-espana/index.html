Rotata becomes a Partner of ZoomInfo in Spain, a key step to transform B2B marketing with AI

In a world where digitalization and artificial intelligence are redefining B2B marketing, Rotata Consulting SL takes a firm step towards the future by becoming an official Partner of ZoomInfo in Spain. Furthermore, this alliance represents a significant boost for companies seeking innovative and scalable solutions.

This strategic alliance reinforces Rotata 's mission to optimize marketing and sales departments through the use of AI-based solutions. Therefore, it significantly improves customer acquisition and data management in medium and large companies.

Why is this alliance key for Spanish companies?

Companies in Spain are experiencing growth in the adoption of advanced marketing tools. However, many still face barriers such as lack of knowledge, lack of accurate data, and difficulty segmenting high-value customers. That's why the combination of Rotata and ZoomInfo makes a crucial difference.

ZoomInfo is a global leader in data intelligence for B2B marketing and sales. In addition, its platform allows access to a detailed and updated database of companies and professionals. This facilitates the identification of prospects with real purchasing intent (Intent Data), automates lead generation and improves decision making with accurate and actionable information.

Benefits of the alliance for companies in Spain

With this collaboration, Rotata will offer its customers:

  • Access to high-quality data: Verified and updated information about prospects and companies in real time. Thus, companies will be able to rely on reliable data for their strategies.
  • Intelligent segmentation: Identification of potential customers with a high probability of conversion, based on purchase intention signals. Consequently, resources are optimized.
  • Sales pipeline automation: Integration with CRM tools and ABM (Account-Based Marketing) strategies to optimize conversion.
  • Greater efficiency in prospecting: Reduction of time and effort in searching for clients, allowing sales teams to focus on closing sales more quickly.

Rotata – ZoomInfo: Leading the digital transformation of B2B marketing

Since its creation, Rotata has positioned itself as a reference consultancy in the AI-First ecosystem. In addition, it has helped companies adopt disruptive technologies to improve their competitiveness in a changing market.

This alliance with ZoomInfo reinforces its commitment to innovation and process optimization in marketing and sales. Therefore, companies will now be able to have a clear competitive advantage in their operations.

«Becoming a Partner of ZoomInfo allows us to bring top-level commercial intelligence tools to Spanish companies. In this way, B2B marketing will be more precise, efficient and based on data,” says Ignacio San Luis Castellano, CEO of Rotata.

The future of B2B marketing is here!

Companies that adopt data intelligence solutions will have a key competitive advantage in a constantly evolving market. Therefore, with this alliance, Rotata and ZoomInfo accelerate the digital transformation of the marketing and sales departments in Spain.

If you want to discover how to make the most of this technology in your company, do not hesitate to contact us.
