Solutions

Intelligence works when signals change the workflow

Account intelligence is useful when data, timing and fit alter who gets attention and what the next action should be.

Review the system See related service
01

Fricción

Data problem

Account intelligence fails when fit, recency and buying-role coverage are missing at the moment of action.

Accounts are prioritized by opinion
Contacts are incomplete at the moment of action
Signals sit outside the workflow
Reps research what the system should already know
02

Modelo operativo

Signal gaps

The gap is rarely a lack of data. It is weak trust, missing context and signals that never change workflow priority.

Fit data exists but is not trusted
Buying roles are missing or stale
Recency does not change priority
Signals do not create tasks or owner changes
03

Capa del sistema

Intelligence layer

Rotata builds an intelligence layer that turns data quality and timing into usable commercial priority.

Enrich accounts

Fill the gaps that block segmentation and coverage.

Score fit

Separate high-fit accounts from background noise.

Detect signals

Make intent and engagement part of the account readout.

Push priority into workflow

Let the signal change routing, tasking or timing.

Prueba

Insights generation

Useful intelligence produces clear readouts the team can act on immediately.

High-fit accounts rise faster
Buying-role gaps become visible
Signal recency changes queue priority
Research time drops before outreach

Modelo operativo

Outcomes

The result is a workflow that knows more before the team spends time, budget or attention.

More usable account context
Stronger weekly review discipline

Higher confidence in prioritization
