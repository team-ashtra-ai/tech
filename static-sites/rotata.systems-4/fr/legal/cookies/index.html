Cookie policy

This page explains how the site uses browser storage, necessary cookies and consent-controlled third-party scripts.

Manage preferences Privacy policy
01

Contexte

Introduction

rotata.es uses a mix of necessary browser storage and optional third-party scripts that only activate after consent where applicable.

02

Contexte

What cookies are

Cookies are small browser files. The site also uses local browser storage to remember consent choices and keep the experience consistent.

03

Modèle opératoire

Types of cookies used

The consent model is organized into clear categories.

Necessary
Analytics
Marketing
Preferences
04

Modèle opératoire

Purpose of cookies

Storage and scripts are used only for specific operational reasons.

Keep the site running
Remember consent choices
Measure usage where analytics consent exists
Load marketing providers only when consent exists
05

Modèle opératoire

Third-party cookies

Third-party tools are only relevant when they are configured and consent allows them.

Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics
Microsoft Clarity
LinkedIn Insight Tag
HubSpot scripts or forms integrations
06

Modèle opératoire

Cookie duration

Duration depends on the storage type and provider involved.

Consent choices remain until changed or cleared
Third-party providers apply their own durations
Necessary storage is kept only as long as needed
Users should review provider policies for specifics
07

Processus

Consent management

The site gives users direct control over non-essential categories.

01

A consent banner appears until a choice is stored

02

Accept all enables analytics, marketing and preferences

03

Necessary only keeps essential storage and behaviour

04

Consent can be reset by clearing browser storage

08

Modèle opératoire

How to disable cookies

Users can disable or limit storage through several routes.

Reject non-essential categories in the banner
Block or clear cookies in browser settings
Clear local storage for a fresh consent choice
Use stricter browser controls where appropriate
09

Contexte

Updates to policy

This policy changes when the storage logic, configured providers or consent model changes.
