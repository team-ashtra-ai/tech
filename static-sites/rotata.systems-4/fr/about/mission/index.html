About

The mission is to make B2B growth governable

Rotata is built for companies that have enough tools, enough activity and still not enough operating clarity.

Review the system See cases
01

Friction

Market gap

Most companies do not lack activity. They lack one operating model that makes activity commercially useful.

Point tools expand faster than system design
Data governance is treated as cleanup
Teams inherit different definitions
Reporting stays downstream from decisions
02

Contexte

Our belief

Growth becomes defendable when teams can trace demand from signal to pipeline movement inside one operating model.

03

Couche système

Vision

The aim is not more complexity. The aim is more control.

One readable system

Teams should understand what is happening without assembling separate stories.

Shared definitions

Qualification and stage logic should stay consistent across functions.

Shorter learning loops

Signals should move into action faster and come back into review sooner.

Better use of platforms

Technology should reinforce the model rather than create parallel realities.

04

Modèle opératoire

What we are building

Rotata builds the layers that make structured growth sustainable.

Reusable architecture patterns
Service layers around one model
Reporting logic tied to action
Decision rhythms that keep the system current
05

Modèle opératoire

Positioning

Rotata is not framed as a generic agency because the work is not campaign delivery by default.

Not a reseller-first business
Not an isolated ops shop
Not a campaign factory
A systems company connecting revenue work end to end
06

Preuve

Long-term impact

When the system is right, performance becomes less dependent on individual heroics.

Faster onboarding
More credible forecast conversations
Better investment decisions
Commercial knowledge stays in the system
