Services

Outbound works when targeting and timing are structured

Outbound is useful when it behaves like a controlled operating motion rather than a campaign burst.

Review the system See related solution
01

Contexto

Role in the system

Outbound creates repeatable market coverage when ICP, timing, message logic and CRM feedback already share one model.

02

Modelo operativo

Outreach structure

Outbound should define who gets coverage, when it starts and how responses return to the system.

ICP and account selection
Role-based audience design
Ownership and reply routing
Measurement linked back to CRM
03

Capa del sistema

Messaging logic

Messaging should reflect account context, role pressure and a clear commercial next step.

Problem-led framing

Anchor outreach in the issue the buyer is already dealing with.

Role context

Change the angle based on the stakeholder receiving the message.

Timing context

Let signals and recency shape when the message appears.

Clear next step

Ask for one concrete action the buyer can evaluate quickly.

04

Modelo operativo

Channels

Channels should work as one motion, not as disconnected activity bursts.

Email for direct problem framing
LinkedIn for context and follow-up
Phone for urgency and clarification
CRM tasks to keep ownership visible
05

Proceso

Sequences

The sequence should breathe. It should change by account context and stop cleanly when the next action changes.

01

Launch with the right account and role context

02

Adjust timing by signal, reply or inactivity

03

Route engagement back to the right owner

04

Stop, continue or escalate based on the readout

06

Prueba

Outputs

The output should be visible in live commercial use.

Higher reply relevance
Cleaner attribution
More consistent follow-up
Better learning from sequence performance
