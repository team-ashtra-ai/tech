About

Rotata is built around operating clarity

Rotata exists to turn disconnected revenue work into one commercial system people can trust.

Review the system See the process
01

Contexto

Who we are

Rotata is a systems company for B2B teams that need revenue operations to become legible, repeatable and commercially useful.

02

Capa del sistema

What we do

Rotata designs the structure that lets tools and teams operate coherently.

Commercial architecture

Define lifecycle, pipeline, ownership and stage logic before automation.

CRM and data structure

Align objects, fields and routing rules to real decisions.

Workflows and handoffs

Make revenue work move through one operating rhythm.

Reporting discipline

Make dashboards read the same model the team uses every day.

03

Fricción

Why it matters

Most revenue friction comes from systems added in parts and never made to behave as one.

Volume rises while qualification stays weak
Automation runs without a reliable rule
Teams debate definitions instead of reading one model
The tool stack grows while visibility gets worse
04

Modelo operativo

How we think

System design comes first because every later decision depends on it.

Start from the real sales motion
Model inputs before automation
Use reporting for decisions
Document the operating logic
05

Capa del sistema

Differentiation

The difference is how the work is framed and controlled.

System before tool

Platforms are chosen for fit inside the architecture.

Cross-functional by default

Marketing, sales, CRM, data and reporting are designed together.

Built for operators

The output has to work for the people using it every day.

Proof over promises

Claims stay tied to structure and visible commercial movement.

06

Prueba

Trust signals

Trust comes from disciplined operating work.

Documented operating model
Named workflow ownership
Metrics tied to pipeline movement
No reseller-first framing

Talk through the current setup

Consulta

Rotata turns fragmented revenue operations into one operating model that marketing and sales can read, run and improve.
