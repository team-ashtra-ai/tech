Privacy policy

This page explains how Rotata handles personal data collected through the site, contact forms and consent-aware analytics setup.

Contexte

Introduction

This policy covers personal data collected through rotata.es and related direct contact channels.

02

Modèle opératoire

Data controller details

Rotata is responsible for the processing described on this page.

Controller: Rotata Consulting SL
Website: rotata.es
Email: info@rotata.com
Base of operations: Spain

Modèle opératoire

Data collected

Rotata keeps collection limited to what is relevant for the contact and operating context.

Contact and company details
Website and message content
UTM and referrer information
Analytics data only where consent allows it

Processus

How data is collected

Data enters through direct user action and consent-aware technical flows.

01

Direct form submission

02

03

Configured analytics or marketing scripts when consent allows it

Couche système

Purpose of processing

Each processing purpose should tie back to a clear business reason.

Respond to inquiries

Read the issue and answer with next steps.

Assess fit

Understand the operating context before proposing a relevant conversation.

Improve the site

Use consented analytics to understand usage and improvement needs.

Protect the site

Use basic anti-spam and operational controls.

06

Modèle opératoire

Legal basis

The legal basis depends on the context of the processing.

Consent
Pre-contractual steps at user request
Legitimate interest in site security and continuity
Legal obligations where required
07

Modèle opératoire

Data sharing

Rotata limits sharing to processors or providers needed to run the service.

Hosting and deployment providers
Configured analytics or marketing providers when consent exists
Form endpoint or CRM processors
Professional service providers under confidentiality duties
08

Modèle opératoire

Data retention

Retention follows necessity, contractual context and legal obligations.

Inquiry data kept as long as needed for the conversation
Analytics retention depends on provider settings
Consent choices remain until changed or cleared
Records may be retained longer where law requires it

Access
Rectification or deletion
Restriction or objection
Portability and withdrawal of consent
